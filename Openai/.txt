## Options for AI-Focused Resignation Emails: 

Due to ethical considerations and potential misunderstandings, directly stating AI as the reason for your resignation isn't recommended. However, you can subtly allude to it or focus on the aspects that drew you to AI:

**Option 1 (Subtle):**

> Subject: Resignation - Your Name
>
> Dear [Boss's name],
>
> Please accept this email as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as [Your position] at [Company name]. My last day of employment will be [Your last day].
>
> This decision comes as I embark on a new path that aligns with my evolving interests in technology and innovation. I am grateful for the experiences and knowledge I've gained at [Company Name], which have prepared me for this next chapter.
>
> Thank you for the opportunities and support during my time here. I wish you and the company continued success.
>
> Sincerely,
> [Your name]

**Option 2 (Focus on Skills):**

> Subject: Resignation - Your Name
>
> Dear [Boss's name],
>
> I am writing to inform you of my decision to resign from my position as [Your position] at [Company name], with my last day being [Your last day].
>
> My time at [Company Name] has allowed me to develop strong skills in [List relevant skills, e.g., data analysis, problem-solving, automation] which I am eager to apply in a new context focused on emerging technologies and their potential impact.
>
> I am thankful for the experiences and the support I received during my time here. I wish you and the company all the best for the future.
>
> Sincerely,
> [Your name] 
***

**Additional Tips:**

* **Maintain professionalism:** Regardless of the reason, keep the tone respectful and professional.
* **Express gratitude:** Acknowledge the positive aspects of your time at the company.
* **Offer assistance with the transition:**  If you're willing, offer to help with training your replacement or documenting your work.
* **Avoid negativity:** Don't criticize the company, your boss, or colleagues. 

**Remember to replace the bracketed information with your specific details.**
